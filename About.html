Discover Our Story: Crafting Spaces, Creating Memories

Experienced Architect

Welcome to Gruhashobha Interiors, where innovation meets craftsmanship and vision transforms spaces. With roots dating back to 1998, we embarked on our journey in the interior industry specializing in fabrication works. Over the years, we've evolved, expanded, and diversified, catering to a wide spectrum of clients and projects across India.
From humble beginnings crafting bespoke interior pieces in our manufacturing plant, we've grown into a dynamic force, offering comprehensive interior solutions for residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces. Our portfolio proudly showcases collaborations with esteemed names like The Orchid Hotels, Ritz-Carlton Pune, and Stauff India Pvt. Ltd., among others.
